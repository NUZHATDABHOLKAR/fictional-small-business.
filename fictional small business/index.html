10 Tips for designing a website a small business

Avoid jumbled website

Keep your website neat with precise information, don't crowd it with too

much content

Know your Aim

Have a good idea about your objectives & what you're trying to

achieve through your website

Insert Keywords

Insert keywords in the potential areas of your website for better search

engine rankings

Have Unique Web Design

Have a unique website design, one that reflects your brand value and

personality

Easy to Navigate Website

Your website must be easily navigable and great user interface for your

visitors to enjoy it

Professional Appearance

Make your website appear professional by using certain professional

colours and imagery

Original & Unique Content

Your website must offer unique, original and informative content

Convey your Message

Convey your message clearly; let your visitors know exactly the

solutions you offer

Showcase your Services

Showcase products and services that clearly reflect the purpose of

your business or brand

Website Must be Accessible
